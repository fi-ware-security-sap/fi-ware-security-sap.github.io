The Security research contributions developed by SAP team in FI-WARE

Welcome to the FI-WARE Security research prototypes developed by SAP.

This space contains two software developed under the umbrella of the FI-WARE project, supported by the European Commission. The FI-WARE project, in a nutshell, "aims to provide a framework for development of smart applications in the Future Internet", please see the FI-WARE Vision page for more details and an explanation of main concepts.

Software

The project hosted on this space are:

  • PPL, an access/usage control service for the cloud.
  • DBA, a service to evaluate anonymization policies and anonymize datasets.
